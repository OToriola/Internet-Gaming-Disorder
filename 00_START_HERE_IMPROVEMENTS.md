## üí° TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Copying Tables
- Markdown tables can be pasted directly into Word
- Or use online markdown-to-Word converter
- Maintain consistent decimal places (e.g., all 0.9765, not mixed)
- Include table notes explaining abbreviations (SD, N, etc.)

### Adding Figures
- Ensure all figures are 300+ DPI for print quality
- Add descriptive captions (what it shows, why it matters)
- Reference all figures in text ("as shown in Figure X...")
- Maintain consistent font sizes across figures

### Writing About Results
- Don't claim "perfect model" - say "100% test accuracy (validated by CV: 97.65%)"
- Don't say "fair across all demographics" - say "consistent across age groups; sex analysis limited by sample size"
- Do explain why perfect accuracy occurred (small positive case count in test set)
- Do highlight clinical implications of threshold choice
